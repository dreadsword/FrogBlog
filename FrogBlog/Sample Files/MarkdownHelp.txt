
**Markdown Help**

Markdown is a plain text format that gets transformed into HTML.
You write your article in Markdown and FrogBlog transforms it into
HTML during the publishing process. You can review how your article
will look in a web browser by pressing the **Preview** button. You can
copy and paste this help document into an article to try this **Preview**
feature.
